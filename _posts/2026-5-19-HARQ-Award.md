Dr. Chaohan Cui to lead new multi-institute team.
---

Dr. Chaohan Cui, Assistant Research Faculty in the Photonic Quantum Systems Group (PhoQuS), has been awarded a $2M two-year DARPA Heterogeneous Architectures for Quantum (HARQ) grant.
He leads a multi-institute team developing an on-chip high-dimensional programmable time-domain photonic quantum processor for high-speed interconnects between heterogeneous quantum processing units.

Read more about the award [at this link.](https://ece.umd.edu/news/story/ece-research-scientist-chaohan-cui-awarded-darpa-award)
