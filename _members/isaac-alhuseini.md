Isaac earned a B.A. in Physics and Mathematics from the University of Colorado Boulder, where he conducted research with the Nesbitt group at JILA. He is pursuing his Ph.D. in the Physics Department at the University of Maryland. His research focuses on quantum networks and quantum communication with diamond color centers and quantum frequency conversion. Outside of research, he enjoys hiking and cooking.
