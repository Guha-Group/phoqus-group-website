Tannishtha Saha is a Postbaccalaureate Research Assistant at the Joint Quantum Institute (JQI) and the PhoQuS group, currently supporting the HARQ-DARPA program. She holds a B.S. in Applied Physics from the University of Maryland, College Park. 

Currently, her work focuses on the development and testing of RF electronics and hardware control systems for quantum physics experimentation.

Outside of work, she enjoys playing golf, badminton, and singing.
