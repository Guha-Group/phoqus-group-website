Diandrea Campbell is an Administrative Coordinator with the Photonic Quantum Systems Group (PhoQuS) within the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Maryland. A Maryland native and UMD alumni, she brings a strong background in administrative operations, program coordination, and organizational management, with experience supporting complex, fast-paced environments across higher education and government settings. In her role with PhoQuS, she provides comprehensive administrative support to faculty, researchers, and collaborators, helping to ensure day-to-day operations, effective coordination, and compliance with university policies and procedures. Diandrea is committed to supporting innovative research initiatives by fostering organized, responsive, and well-managed administrative processes that allow research teams to focus on advancing scientific discovery.
